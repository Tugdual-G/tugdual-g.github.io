About

This website aims to present some of my works and reflections, mainly revolving around scientific computing, maths and physics.

Amongst the sources of joy and excitement in my life are the discovery of new topics and skills, nature either through contemplation or physical activity, and music in its diversity.

I'm an obsessive problem-solver when it comes to creating and crafting things, I love and need to totally immerse myself in a topic when it picks up my interest.

Wooden spoons

I like to sculpt green wood with an hatchet and a knife to make wooden spoons.

European Trees

I'm quite interested in indigenous trees and learnt to recognize most of them. Below is an hybrid oak (Quercus Ilex Quercus Pyrenaica) three raised from the seed that I collected during my bike trip.

Bicycle trip

After using my bike for commuting for a while, I realised that it could be easy to cycle to Spain. I choose to go to the Systema Central. I sewed bags for the frame, put a tent older than me back into service, and after saving a little money I took the opportunity to go. I never really had the occasion to travel, so it was a blast.
